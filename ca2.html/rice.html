Rice Crop Protection

Common Pesticides

  • Chlorpyrifos - Organophosphate insecticide
  • Carbofuran - Systemic insecticide and nematicide
  • Malathion - Organophosphate insecticide

Detection Methods

  • Chromatographic techniques: HPLC, GC-MS for accurate residue analysis
  • Immunoassay tests: Rapid field detection kits
  • Biosensor detection: Enzyme-based sensors for specific pesticides
  • Spectroscopic methods: FTIR and Raman spectroscopy
  • Colorimetric tests: Simple visual detection methods

Additional Protection Tips

  • Practice proper water management to reduce pest habitats
  • Use resistant rice varieties when available
  • Implement crop rotation with non-host crops
  • Monitor fields regularly for early pest detection
