Vegetable Crop Protection

Common Pesticides

  • Imidacloprid - Neonicotinoid insecticide
  • Cypermethrin - Pyrethroid insecticide
  • Mancozeb - Dithiocarbamate fungicide

Organic Solutions

  • Neem oil: Effective against many pests and fungi
  • Diatomaceous earth: Physical insecticide that damages exoskeletons
  • Bacillus thuringiensis (Bt): Natural bacterial insecticide
  • Companion planting: Strategic plant combinations that deter pests
  • Homemade sprays: Garlic, chili or soap-based sprays

Additional Protection Tips

  • Practice good sanitation in the garden
  • Use floating row covers as physical barriers
  • Encourage beneficial insects with flowering plants
  • Rotate vegetable families each season
  • Monitor plants regularly for early signs of problems
