Wheat Crop Protection

Common Pesticides

  • Glyphosate - Broad-spectrum systemic herbicide
  • 2,4-D - Selective systemic herbicide
  • Dicamba - Broadleaf herbicide

Safe Alternatives

  • Integrated Pest Management (IPM): Combines biological, cultural and chemical methods
  • Biological controls: Use of natural predators and parasites
  • Organic herbicides: Vinegar-based or citric acid solutions
  • Crop rotation: With legumes or other non-cereal crops
  • Mechanical weeding: Flame weeding or cultivation

Additional Protection Tips

  • Monitor fields for disease symptoms weekly
  • Use certified disease-free seeds
  • Adjust planting dates to avoid peak pest periods
  • Maintain proper plant spacing for air circulation
