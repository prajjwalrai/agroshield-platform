Pesticide Safety Spotlight

Chlorpyrifos Safety Alert

Recent studies show concerning neurotoxic effects from this commonly used pesticide. Many countries have banned its agricultural use.

Health Risks: Linked to developmental issues in children and neurological damage
Common Crops: Corn, soybeans, fruit trees, and some vegetables
Safer Alternatives: Neem oil, Spinosad, or Bacillus thuringiensis (Bt)

If you suspect Chlorpyrifos contamination, contact our experts for testing and soil remediation recommendations.
