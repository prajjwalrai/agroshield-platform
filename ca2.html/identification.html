Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

We can identify a wide range of pesticides including organophosphates, carbamates, pyrethroids, neonicotinoids, and many others. Our laboratory is equipped with advanced analytical instruments capable of detecting most commercially available agricultural pesticides.

After submitting your request, we'll send detailed sampling instructions based on your specific situation. Generally, you should collect affected plant parts in clean containers, avoid contamination, and ship with ice packs if necessary. We recommend using our prepaid shipping labels for proper handling.

Standard analysis is completed within 3-5 business days after sample receipt. Rush services are available for an additional fee (results in 24-48 hours). Complex cases requiring additional testing may take longer, but we'll keep you informed of any delays.

Your comprehensive report will include: identified pesticides and concentrations, comparison with safety standards, potential sources of contamination, risk assessment, and recommended actions. We also provide consultation to help interpret the results.
